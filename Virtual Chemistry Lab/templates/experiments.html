🔬 Chemistry Experiments

Explore and perform safe, interactive simulations of common chemistry experiments.

🌊 True Solution Prep

Make clear, transparent solution by dissolving salt, sugar, or alum in water.

🔍 Salt + Sand Separation

Use filtration and evaporation to separate components.

🌫 Sublimation of NH₄Cl

Watch solid turn into gas directly on heating, then re-solidify.

🧪 Paper Chromatography

Separate dyes/pigments based on solubility in solvent.

🧼 Chalk Filtration

Remove insoluble chalk from water using filter paper.

🔁 Iron + Copper Sulphate

Iron displaces copper showing color and metal change.

🔥 Burning Magnesium

Bright white flame forms white powder (MgO).

🥼 Heating Ferrous Sulphate

Color changes and gas smell confirm decomposition.

🌀 Heating CuSO₄ Crystals

Blue hydrated crystals turn white on heating, regain blue on adding water.

🧁 Baking Soda + Vinegar

Produces CO₂ gas bubbles, inflates balloon.

🧱 Combination Reaction

Any 2 reactants combine to form 1 product.

⚡ Decomposition (KMnO₄)

Heat splits potassium permanganate into simpler products.

🔄 Displacement Reaction

Iron replaces copper from CuSO₄, forming brown layer.

🧂 Double Displacement

Two salt solutions form a white precipitate.

🧪 Acid + Metal (Zn + HCl)

Acid reacts with metal to release hydrogen gas.

🧼 CO₂ Test with Limewater

CO₂ from acid-carbonate mix turns limewater milky.

🔵 pH of Substances

Test lemon, soap, cola with universal indicator strip.

⚖ Neutralization (HCl + NaOH)

Acid + base → salt + water, heat is released.

🌩 Salt Reactions with AgNO₃ / BaCl₂

White/cream precipitate confirms presence of ions.

🔥 Thermal Decomposition

Compounds break into simpler ones using heat.

🌧 Rusting of Iron

Wet air causes iron to form brown flaky coating.

🧱 Heating Copper in Air

Black layer forms due to copper oxide.

🧴 Indicator Reaction

Observe color changes with litmus, phenolphthalein.

🔥 Reaction of Sodium with Water

Observe the exothermic reaction of sodium metal with water and the formation of hydrogen gas.

💧 Acid-Base Indicator

Use litmus paper and phenolphthalein to test the pH of different solutions.

🧪 Preparation of Hydrogen

Simulate the reaction between zinc and dilute hydrochloric acid to prepare hydrogen gas.

⚗️ Crystallization of Copper Sulphate

Learn how to purify a substance by crystallizing copper sulphate from its solution.

🌡️ Heating Effect of Current

Simulate the experiment demonstrating Joule’s Law using a coil and current source.
